Dear Parents,
Welcome to PBC Cricket Academy, Perth. We will run & manage our Junior cricket competitions and Training from this academy and will be supported by Perth Bengal Club Inc and collaboration with WACA. Our all kids will be
registered via My Cricket and will get Insurance we required.

Main objective: Give more opportunity to our kids to gain confidence & Win the challenge. Our objective is to create a competitive match scenario where young players can learn and adopt the match situation. We will ensure the equal opportunity for each players. There will be assessment after the competition and progress report will be
given to the parents as part of the continuous improvement. There will be awards and individual
prize money for the top performers

Please submit the form if you are interested to give your own team any age group or if you want to register your kids to the competition. Or feel free to call if you have any further inquiries or questions.

How to submit the form?
Please select the age group you are interested (can select more than one if needed) and then follow the section. Please click 'Next' if the section is not relevant for you and fill up the details for the section is relevant for you. Thank you.
